About Mary G.

With over 15 years of professional experience, Mary G. is a versatile traditional artist based in the United Arab Emirates. Specializing in the demanding realm of Hyperrealism, her work captures the fine balance between technical precision and emotional depth.

Her mastery extends beyond a single medium, encompassing Charcoal, Color Pencil, Acrylic, Oil Painting, and Mix Media. Each piece is a meticulous journey through detail, light, and shadow, reflecting a lifelong dedication to the craft of fine art.
