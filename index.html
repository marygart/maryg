About Mary G.

With over 15 years of professional experience, Mary G. is a versatile traditional artist based in the United Arab Emirates, specializing in the demanding realm of Hyperrealism. While her mastery often shines through colored pencils, her expertise spans across various traditional media and artistic styles. Her work is a testament to patience and profound observation, capturing the essence of her subjects through meticulous details that challenge the boundary between art and reality.
